Data Sources

Comprehensive Environmental Intelligence

Our platform integrates multiple NASA Earth observation datasets with ground-based sensors to provide comprehensive environmental monitoring and forecasting capabilities.

NASA TEMPO

Tropospheric Emissions: Monitoring of Pollution

Resolution: 2.1 km x 4.5 km Coverage: North America Frequency: Hourly measurements Pollutants: NO2, O3, SO2, HCHO

NASA MODIS

Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer

Resolution: 250m - 1km Coverage: Global Frequency: Daily Data: Land surface temperature, vegetation

NASA VIIRS

Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite

Resolution: 375m - 750m Coverage: Global Frequency: Daily Data: Night lights, fires, clouds

Ground Sensors

Urban Environmental Monitoring Network

Sensors: 10,000+ deployed Coverage: Major cities worldwide Frequency: Real-time Data: PM2.5, PM10, CO, temperature

Weather Stations

Meteorological Data Integration

Stations: 15,000+ global Coverage: Worldwide Frequency: Hourly updates Data: Wind, humidity, pressure

Citizen Science

Crowdsourced Environmental Data

Contributors: 100,000+ Coverage: Urban areas Frequency: Continuous Data: Local observations, photos
