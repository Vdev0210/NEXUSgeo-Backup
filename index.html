Smart Pathways for Healthy Cities

We combine NASA Earth Observation data with urban planning intelligence to enable climate-resilient, people-first cities. Includes a secondary real-time air-quality forecasting feature powered by NASA TEMPO and ground sensors.

24/7 Monitoring
99.9% Accuracy
50TB+ Data Daily

Our Mission

Climate change and rapid urbanization threaten city wellbeing. Our platform turns NASA Earth data into practical planning actions: mapping growth, identifying vulnerabilities, and forecasting air quality so city leaders and communities can act before harm occurs.

Data Sources

Our platform integrates data from multiple NASA satellite missions and Earth observation programs

MODIS

Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer

Resolution: 250m - 1km Coverage: Global Data Types: Temperature, Vegetation, Fires

Landsat 8

Land remote sensing satellite

Resolution: 30m Coverage: Global Data Types: Multispectral imagery

Sentinel-2

ESA's Earth observation satellite

Resolution: 10m - 60m Coverage: Global Data Types: High-resolution optical imagery

VIIRS

Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite

Resolution: 375m - 750m Coverage: Global Data Types: Night lights, Fires, Clouds
